No Contracts

Save up to $10/month per family member by signing up for AutoRenew. AutoRenew allows automatic tuition payment every month and automatic annual registration fee payment via your credit or debit card. AutoRenew is Enso Center's preferred method of payment.

Annual Fee: The annual registration fee is $50 for an individual or $75 for a family. The annual registration fee is tax-deductible. Only one registration fee per year regardless of number of classes or arts attended.

Family discounts apply to family members with Full Martial Arts, Hapkido or Martial Arts FUNdamentals Packages. Discounts are applied to packages in order of MOST expensive to LEAST expensive.

For example: Dad has a Full Martial Arts package. Mom has a Hapkido package. Both of their children have Martial Arts FUNdamentals packages.

Dad is the 1st family member and pays the full rate for his Full Martial Arts package.
Mom is the 2nd family member and gets 25% off her Hapkido package.
Both children are considered additional family members and each gets 50% off their Martial Arts FUNdamentals package.
